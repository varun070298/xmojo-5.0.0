About XMOJO


XMOJO was formed as a foundation to provide high quality software and implementation of standards with open and collaborative development and effective technical-based interactions. At XMOJO, we ensure that resources donated by companies and individuals will be used for the public benefit.

The XMOJO project for JMX is the first project under XMOJO umbrella. This implementation is derived from AdventNet's JMX implementation, which has been available in the industry since 1999.

XMOJO is sponsored by AdventNet Inc. (www.adventnet.com), the leading provider of enterprise and carrier-grade solutions used for rapid development and deployment of end-to-end network and application management solutions.
